**Confidence (secondary; H4; 7-point Likert).** After each comprehension item Q1-Q4, participants rated their confidence (1 = not at all confident, 7 = completely confident). Q5 is open-ended and receives no confidence rating. Confidence composite = mean across Q1-Q4. Used as the primary measure for H4: confirmatory one-way ANOVA on confidence composite across conditions, with Tukey HSD for B vs. A, C, D (m = 3 Holm family; §4.5). Also used as input to the pre-registered H4 calibration analysis: per-participant Spearman rank correlation between Q1-Q4 accuracy score (0-4) and confidence composite, computed per condition and reported descriptively (pre-registered secondary/descriptive; not in the m = 3 Holm family; pre-reg §6.7; see §4.5).
